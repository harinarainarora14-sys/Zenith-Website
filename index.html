About Zenith

A youth-led force for good, rooted in Bareilly and guided by the Rotary ideal of service above self.

Our Mission

To create positive, lasting impact in Bareilly through service, education, and community engagement, while developing the skills and character of every member.

Our Vision

A community where young leaders take initiative, collaborate with compassion, and build a more inclusive and sustainable future.

Our Story

Interact Club Zenith was founded by Ayaan and Hari Narain Arora, two students who believed that young people could drive meaningful change. Established on 23 March 2026 and chartered on 30 March 2026, the club quickly grew into a diverse team of around 30 passionate volunteers.

Under the guidance of Club Advisor Rotarian Vishal Arora, and in close partnership with Rotary Club South Bareilly, Zenith has launched initiatives focused on environmental health, public health awareness, and community wellness.

Rotary Connection

As part of the global Interact network sponsored by Rotary International, Zenith follows the ideal of service above self. Our work is supported and guided by Rotary Club South Bareilly.

Teams You Can Join

No specific skills required — just the willingness to contribute and grow.

Management

The operational backbone of Zenith. You will coordinate across teams, translate ideas into action, and ensure every initiative runs smoothly.

Creative

Design posters, banners, certificates, and branding materials that represent Zenith visually. If you enjoy design and visual storytelling, this is your team.

Social Media

Create content, shoot reels, edit videos, and grow Zenith's online presence. If you have an eye for content and a passion for storytelling, this is for you.

Finance

Handle budgeting, expense tracking, and financial records for Zenith's events and operations. Detail-oriented and reliable — that's all you need.
