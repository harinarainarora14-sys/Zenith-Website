Founded 2026

Our Story

Interact Club Zenith was founded by Ayaan and Hari Narain Arora, two students who believed that young people could drive meaningful change in their community.

Established on 23 March 2026 and formally chartered on 30 March 2026, the club quickly grew into a diverse team of around 30 passionate volunteers, united by a shared purpose.

Under the guidance of Club Advisor Rotarian Vishal Arora, and in close partnership with Rotary Club South Bareilly, Zenith has already launched multiple initiatives focused on environmental health, public health awareness, and community wellness — all within its first three months.

Rotary Connection

On the ground

Our Events

Action that speaks louder than words.

Completed 22 April 2026 · World Earth Day

BLOOM 🌸

Delhi Public School, Bareilly

  • Distributed 600 Tulsi saplings and flowering plants to students.
  • Planted 5 permanent trees on campus.
  • Educated 200–300 students through hands-on activities and awareness sessions.
  • Distributed reusable cloth bags to educators.
  • Taught students about the medicinal and ecological benefits of Tulsi.
Completed 31 May 2026 · World No Tobacco Day

BREATH 🚭

Dentwayy Clinic, Bareilly · In partnership with Dentwayy

  • Art competition and oath-taking ceremony for attendees.
  • Awareness session led by expert dentists on tobacco's health effects.
  • Community pamphlet distribution drive across the locality.
  • Certificates and awareness kits distributed to all participants.
Completed 21 June 2026 · International Yoga Day

YOGA DAY 🧘

Sun City Colony, Bareilly · 6:30 AM

Community yoga session open to all ages. Family-friendly morning event celebrating health and mindfulness.
